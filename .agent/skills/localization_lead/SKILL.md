### 1. The "Tone-Aware Translation"

When translating an article:

1. **Read for Meaning:** Understand the *metaphor* first.
2. **Translate:** Convert to Turkish.
    * *Rule:* Use active voice.
    * *Rule:* Avoid excessive "Plaza Turkish" (English-Turkish mix) unless necessary for technical terms (Sprint, Scrum).
    * *Rule:* Ensure metaphors (Lighthouse, Warrior) are translated consistently.
3. **Localize:** Adjust examples.
    * *Example:* If the English text mentions "Thanksgiving," change it to a generic "Holiday" or a culturally relevant equivalent if the context permits.

### 2. The "Title Remix"

Turkish audiences react different to headlines. Create 3 options:

1. **Direct:** Accurate translation.
2. **Emotional:** Focus on the feeling (e.g., "Why your team is silent").
3. **Professional:** "Kurumsal" style for LinkedIn.
