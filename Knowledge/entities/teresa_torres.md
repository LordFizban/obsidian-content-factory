# Teresa Torres (Continuous Discovery Habits)

## Summary
Product discovery expert and author of *Continuous Discovery Habits*. She defined the modern framework for how product trios (PM, Design, Engineering) should continuously validate assumptions with customers rather than building blindly.

## Relevance
- **Domain alignment:** The definitive source for the **AI in Scrum** pillar (using AI for discovery) and the **Manager Partnership** pillar (co-designing with stakeholders).
- **Source of frameworks:** Provides structural tools to combat the "Decision Fog" by mapping opportunities visually before jumping to solutions.
