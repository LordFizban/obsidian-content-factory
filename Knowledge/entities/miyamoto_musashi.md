# Miyamoto Musashi (A Book of Five Rings)

## Summary
The undefeated 17th-century Japanese swordsman. *A Book of Five Rings* is his defining text on strategy, written at the end of his life. It represents the ultimate transition from a pure tactician (obsessed with technique) to a global strategist (obsessed with perception, adaptability, and systems).

## Relevance
- **Domain alignment:** Philosophical foundation for the **Psychological Safety** and **Manager Partnership** pillars.
- **Source of frameworks:** Provides the perfect historical metaphor for Kaan's transition from the "Warrior" (forcing change) to the "Statesman" (creating conditions for change).
