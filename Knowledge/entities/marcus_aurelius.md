# Marcus Aurelius (Meditations)

## Summary
The Roman Emperor and Stoic philosopher. *Meditations* is his personal diary written during a period of intense crisis (war, plague, and political betrayal). It is the ultimate manual for leading through chaos by focusing exclusively on what one can control.

## Relevance
- **Domain alignment:** The foundational text for Kaan's **Psychological Safety** and **Lighthouse Leadership** pillars.
- **Source of frameworks:** Validates the idea that a Scrum Master's true value isn't enforcing process, but providing an unshakable, objective anchor when the sprint hits turbulence.
