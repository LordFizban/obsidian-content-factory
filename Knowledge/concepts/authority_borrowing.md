# Authority Borrowing

## Definition

Referencing, reposting, or tagging established thought leaders to leverage their audience reach and credibility. Under [[360_brew]], this triggers cross-network distribution via the tagged person's engagement graph. The mechanism works because meaningful engagement from a high-follower account exposes the post to their network.

## Current Rule

- Authority Borrowing is the **only proven follower conversion mechanism** (Q1 2026).
- Schedule 1 Authority Borrowing post every 2 weeks.
- **Cooling policy:** Max 2 references per person per quarter.
- Currently cooled: [[danish_soomro]], [[fred_deichler]], [[mark_graban]] (all maxed in Q1).
- Q2 scouting pipeline: Jurgen Appelo, Esther Derby, Lyssa Adkins.
- Organizations ([[scrum_org]]) have no cooling limit.

## Evolution
