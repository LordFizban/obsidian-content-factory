# The Obstacle is the Way

## Definition
From Marcus Aurelius: "The impediment to action advances action. What stands in the way becomes the way." The idea that difficulties are not interruptions to our work; they are the work itself.

## Current Rule
- This maps to the **Continuous Improvement** pillar. In Agile, uncovering a blocker (like technical debt or a broken deployment pipeline) is not a delay; making that blocker visible *is* the value of the sprint.
- Tie this into cycle time metrics: resolving the obstacle improves the global flow.
