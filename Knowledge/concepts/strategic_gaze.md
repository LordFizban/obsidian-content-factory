# The Strategic Gaze (Perception vs. Sight)

## Definition
From Musashi's *A Book of Five Rings*: "Perception is strong and sight is weak." Sight looks at the immediate threat (the tip of the enemy's sword). Perception sees the whole dynamic, the opponent's balance, and the terrain.
