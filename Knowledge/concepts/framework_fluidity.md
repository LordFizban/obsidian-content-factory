# Framework Fluidity (No Favorite Weapon)

## Definition
From Musashi's *A Book of Five Rings* (The Book of Water): "Do not have a favorite weapon." The principle of extreme adaptability to the terrain, rather than forcing a predefined solution onto a complex problem.
