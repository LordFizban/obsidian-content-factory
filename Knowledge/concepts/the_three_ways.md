# The Three Ways

## Definition
The underlying principles of DevOps from *The Phoenix Project*:
1. **The First Way (Flow):** Understand and increase the flow of work from Development to IT Operations to the Customer. (Focus on global, not local goals).
2. **The Second Way (Feedback):** Create right-to-left feedback loops that are fast and continuous.
3. **The Third Way (Continual Learning):** Foster a high-trust culture that supports dynamic learning, experimentation, and risk-taking.

## Current Rule
- **The First Way** supports Vertical Slicing and cycle-time metrics (Continuous Improvement).
- **The Second Way** supports Agile retro practices.
- **The Third Way** maps conceptually to the **Psychological Safety** and **Lighthouse Leadership** pillars.
