# Lighthouse Leadership

## Definition

Kaan's primary brand metaphor. The Scrum Master/Agile Coach operates as a **Lighthouse** — a calm, consistent signal during organizational chaos. Not a rescue boat (that's the Warrior), but a fixed, visible reference point teams can orient toward.

**Three properties:**
1. **Visible Operating Rules:** Teams know what to expect.
2. **Steady Pace:** No panic, no hero culture.
3. **Psychological Safety First:** People speak up before problems escalate.

Connected to Stoic philosophy — Marcus Aurelius, the idea of controlling what you can and accepting what you can't.
