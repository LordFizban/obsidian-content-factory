# Outcomes over Outputs

## Definition
The fundamental shift from measuring success by delivery (Output: "We shipped the feature on time") to measuring success by impact (Outcome: "The feature increased user retention by 2%").

## Current Rule
- This is the primary driver of the **Continuous Improvement** pillar.
- Connect this to the concept of "Vertical Slicing." A vertical slice must deliver an *Outcome* (a testable change in behavior) not just an *Output* (a merged pull request).
