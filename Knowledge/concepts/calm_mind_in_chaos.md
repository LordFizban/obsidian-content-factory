# Calm Mind in Chaos

## Definition
Musashi's doctrine that in the frenzy of battle, the mind must remain exactly as calm as in everyday life; and in everyday life, as sharp as in battle. There is no "special" mindset for emergencies.

## Current Rule
- This concept is the philosophical engine of **Lighthouse Leadership**.
- The Scrum Master does not panic when the sprint fails or a production incident occurs. By maintaining the everyday baseline mindset, they provide a safe anchoring point for the team.
