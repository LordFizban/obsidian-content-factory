# The Hero Bottleneck (Brent)

## Definition
From *The Phoenix Project*, "Brent" is the indispensible senior engineer who knows how to fix everything. Because he is continually pulled into Unplanned Work (Type 4) to save the day, he becomes the universal constraint. Everything routes through him, delaying planned work.

## Current Rule
- This is the archetype of the **Warrior mode** gone wrong. The Warrior jumps in to fix it (being the hero), causing the bottleneck.
- The **Statesman mode** involves protecting the bottleneck, isolating them from unplanned work so systemic flow can resume.
- Use the "Brent" archetype when discussing key-person dependencies in the Manager Partnership pillar.
