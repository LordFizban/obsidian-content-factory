# Turkish Content Strategy

## Definition

Publishing Turkish-language content as a reach lever targeting the 67% Turkish-speaking segment of Kaan's LinkedIn audience. Turkish posts drive strong passive reach (impressions) but generate zero comments. The strategy treats this as acceptable — reach is the goal, not conversation.

## Evidence

| Post | Date | Language | Impressions | Reactions | Comments | Notes |
|:---|:---|:---|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---|
| Turkish Kaizen Bridge | 2026-03-06 | Turkish | 549 | 9 | 0 | 2nd best Q1 impressions. Zero comments. |
| Turkish Comment (LinkedIn) | 2026-03-12 | Turkish | ~200 | 3 | 0 | Short-form Turkish comment test. |

## Current Rule

- **Cadence:** 1 Turkish post per month (last Thursday of the month).
- **Source:** Only translate Q1 top performers. Don't create Turkish-original content yet.
- **Translation:** Localization Lead skill applied. Preserve thought leader references, cultural nuance, and core metaphors (Lighthouse, Warrior).
- **Measurement:** Compare against W12 baseline (549 impressions, 9 reactions).
- **Expectation:** Zero comments is acceptable. Reach is enough.

## Evolution

- **Pre-Q1 2026:** No Turkish content strategy.
- **Mar 2026:** First Turkish post (Kaizen Bridge translation). 549 impressions — 2nd best Q1 performance. Zero comments.
- **Apr 2026 (planned):** Turkish "Respect Is Not Being Nice" translation (W17 Thu). Hypothesis: if the Q1 #1 post (653 imps EN) is translated, Turkish version should match or beat the 549 baseline.
