# The Inner Citadel (Locus of Control)

## Definition
From Marcus Aurelius: "You have power over your mind - not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength." The Stoic concept that while external events are uncontrollable (a re-org, scope creep), our rational response to them remains entirely in our control.
