# Continuous Interviewing

## Definition
From Teresa Torres: The habit of a product trio engaging with at least one customer every single week. 

## Evidence
| Post | Date | Result | Supports/Contradicts |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| N/A | 2026-04-04 | Ingested | Source validation |

## Current Rule
- Maps to **Co-Design** and **Time-to-Clarity**. The only way to stop guessing what the business/customer wants is to talk to them constantly.
- Use this concept to contrast against "Big Design Up Front" or disappearing for a 3-month build cycle.
