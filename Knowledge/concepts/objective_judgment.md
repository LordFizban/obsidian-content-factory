# Objective Judgment (Stripping the Narrative)

## Definition
From Marcus Aurelius: The practice of seeing things exactly as they are without adding emotional narratives or value judgments. "Don't tell yourself anything more than what your initial impressions report."

## Current Rule
- This concept cures the **Decision Fog** in the **Manager Partnership** pillar. 
- When teams or managers get trapped in narratives ("They don't trust us," "They are incompetent"), the Statesman strips away the emotion to look at the objective data (e.g. "We have an 8-day wait state approving this PR"). The solution is structural, not personal.
